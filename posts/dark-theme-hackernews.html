Hacker News as a community is amazing. The design of the site is not so pretty. I decided to install hn-speical, the chrome extension . Giving me a fresh, feature-full, Hacker News site. But everything needs a darkside!

I present to you hn-special-dark!

Installation

  1. To install hn-special-dark download then unzip
  2. Go to chrome://extenstions and enable developer mode
  3. Click load unpacked extenstion, and select the hn-special-dark folder
  4. Whoa! You're finished. Enjoy!
